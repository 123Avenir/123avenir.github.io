About Us

Avenir Group, a Hong Kong-based aviation services provider dedicated to delivering comprehensive, high-quality solutions for the global aviation industry.

With extensive experience in airlines, MRO, aviation materials and AOG support, we keep your aircraft safely flying!

Our team combines deep industry knowledge with global resource networks to provide reliable, efficient services tailored to your unique operational needs.

Our Services

Procurement Support

We partner with leading OEMs (Airbus, Boeing) and extensive suppliers across China, providing efficient sourcing and delivery of engine components, HMUs and specialized aviation materials. Our procurement solutions ensure timely availability of critical parts with strict quality control.

Repair Management

We offer repair management via CAAC-certified MRO partners in China, servicing B737, A320 and other aircraft models for landing gears, HMUs, servovalves and more. Our streamlined process ensures fast turnaround times without compromising on quality standards.

Equipment R&D

We collaborate with trusted suppliers to design and manufacture advanced test benches and systems, including hydraulic, pneumatic, fuel, lubrication equipment, HMU and electronic testing solutions. Our R&D team works closely with clients to develop customized equipment that meets specific operational requirements.

From concept design to prototype testing and mass production, we provide end-to-end R&D support to enhance your operational efficiency and reliability.

Consulting & Others

Avenir Group also provides specialized aviation consulting services, leveraging our industry expertise across MRO, airline, and the broader Greater China market. We serve as a strategic gateway for foreign companies seeking to enter or expand in China, offering support in areas such as joint venture or MRO establishment, CAAC/CAMAC certification, and engagement with major and regional airlines. Concurrently, we assist Chinese aviation firms in their global expansion efforts, facilitating their growth into international markets.
